# 🚤 Top-Down 2D Boat Racing Game

## Core Concept
- **Genre:** Top-down 2D racing game
- **Theme:** Boat racing in Amsterdam
- **Track:** Around the office (serves as start & finish line)
- **Multiplayer:** Local multiplayer (shared screen, multiple inputs)
- **No AI opponents** — human players only for now

---

## Controls
- 3 buttons only:
  - Left turn
  - Right turn
  - Steering wheel (throttle/control)
- Natural physical feel when driving
- Motor & voltage as control mechanics

---

## Setting
- Amsterdam environment
- Office = start & finish line
- Canals as race track

---

## Weather / Flood Mechanic
- Wind mechanic affects gameplay
- Extended wind → **Storm triggers**
- During storm: **flooding occurs**
  - Water overflows canal banks
  - Players can move **outside of canals** (on flooded streets)

---

## Flood Power-Ups
- Special power-ups **only available during flood**
- Example locations:
  - **Albert Heijn** (supermarket) — enter to collect power-up
- **Risk/Reward mechanic:**
  - Swim further = better power-ups
  - ⚠️ Risk: flood ends while far away → stranded on shore, can't return in time
